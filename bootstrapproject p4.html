Nairobi TOUR

Nairobi is the capital city of Kenya and a definite wonder to explore. Being the home of several animal orphanages and parks, it is the right place to spend time and resources in without any regrets. There are many travel agencies that have their main headquaters in the beautiful city of Nairobi. Nairobi is also home to numerous hotels and lodgings that will satisfy your needs and wants and will make you feel very much at home.

MOMBASA TOUR

Mombasa city is the only city at the Coast of Kenya and it has numerous exciting sites to visit especially pre-historic ones that will take you aback and enlighten you moreon Kenya's amazing history. The beautiful resorts anb beach are overwhelming and relaxing too. There is a lot of culture in Mombasa and rhe people are very friendly and hospitable.

RIFT VALLEY TOUR

The rift Valley is the largest natural valley in Africa and a beaty you would not want to miss out on. The main occupants are the Kalenjin who mainly and largly practice pastoral farming. It is a rich land full of lots to see. Large scale agricultural farmingis also practised.
